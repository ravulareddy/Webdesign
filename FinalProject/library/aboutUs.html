About the Library

Our Mission

The vision of the Libraries aligns with the mission of the Knowledge Hub. The knowledge hub libraries has world class collections and services. The mission is to help people succeed. This is done by effectively managing and delivering the us of content and technology.

Libraries Factsheet

  • Fine Arts Library opened in 1956 (100,000sq.feet on 6 levels)
  • Engineering Library opened in 1966 (140,000sq.feet on 9 levels)
  • Law Library opened in 1916 (60,000sq.feet on 4 levels)
  • Medical Library opened in 1906 (130,000sq.feet on 8 levels)
  • Management Library opened in 1986 (67,000sq.feet on 3 levels)
